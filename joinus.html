Mentorship Opportunities

Are you a long beach student age 14-18 looking to share your work and receive critical guidance and feedback? We have a directory of artists spanning across artistic disciplines who are vetted who have your back. Our application process involves the submission of a portfolio and a statement of intent. Mentorship pairings are a 1+ year commitment. Interdisciplinary guidance is available.
