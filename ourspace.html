Our Space

Physical Gallery and Community Lab

Our Atlantic Ave. location boasts over 6,000 square feet of space.
1000 sq. feet are dedicated exclusively to our gallery. Exhibits are rotated on a monthly basis and are thematic. Our student-run gallery team chooses each monthly theme, and drives set-up and break-down of all shows.
June's theme is “Recycled Fashion.” Artists 14-18 are invited to submit creative works.

We're always looking for enthusiastic new talent. Reach out to us if you are a student, and we'll get you situated!

Volunteer

One communal performance space, complete with flexible staging and gallery seating is seasonally convertible and supports a range of student offerings. Cozy spaces replete with textiles, canvas, paints, boards, books, pens, pencils and more are free an available for student use.
Drop in and make. Enjoy a good book. Write your next screenplay. Hang out with friends. Host your own arts events. The options are endless.
