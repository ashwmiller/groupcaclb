Our Space

Physical Gallery + Community Lab

Our Atlantic Ave. location boasts over 6,000 square feet of space, 1000 sq. feet dedicated exclusively to our gallery. Our lab is complete with flexible staging and convertible gallery seating, as well as cozy work spaces replete with textiles, canvas, paints, books and more.

Exhibits are curated by students and rotated on a monthly basis. June's theme is “Recycled Fashion.” Artists 14-18 are invited to submit creative works.
