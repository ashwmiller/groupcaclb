WHO WE ARE

Fellow artists

CAC LB is a collaborative, intergenerational community co-creating inclusive and accessible physical and digital spaces for youth to engage in creative exploration and expression.


WHO WE'RE FOR

The next generation.

Welcoming all Long Beach youth looking to explore the arts or amplify their creative expression. The next generation of artistic talent will find a home here at Creative Arts Community Long Beach.


WHY WE'RE HERE

A space for artists, by artists.

Long Beach youth deserve inclusive, accessible spaces that they've co-designed, where they can freely explore creative expression.
Started in 2024, CAC Lb connects Long Beach youth with arts advocates, organizations, and resources with the mission of creating student-inspired, student-led environments of creative permission.
